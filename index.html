AVL Rotation Quick Reference
LL Rotation (Right Rotation): Inserted in left subtree of left child. Fix: rotate right at unbalanced node. New root = left child.
RR Rotation (Left Rotation): Inserted in right subtree of right child. Fix: rotate left at unbalanced node. New root = right child.
LR Rotation (Left-Right): Inserted in right subtree of left child. Fix: first rotate left at left child, then rotate right at unbalanced node.
RL Rotation (Right-Left): Inserted in left subtree of right child. Fix: first rotate right at right child, then rotate left at unbalanced node.
Stack Operator Precedence (Infix → Postfix)
Priority in stack: ^ (3) > *, / (2) > +, - (1) > ( (0)

Rule: Pop from stack while stack top precedence ≥ current operator precedence (except for ^ which is right-associative — pop only if strictly greater).
Parentheses: push ( directly; on ), pop until matching (.
